
    Minimum:
        Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
        OS: Windows 10/11 OR Linux arch64/x86_64
        Processor: AMD / Intel (AVX2 Instruction Set) 
        Memory: 8 GB RAM
        Graphics: Radeon RX 570 (8GB VRAM) / GeForce GTX 1650 (4GB VRAM)
	Storage: 10 GB GB available space
	Notes: On Linux, it is recommended that a SWAP partition of 8GB should be used.
